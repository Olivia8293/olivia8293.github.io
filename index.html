This is my fun website, I am Gertrude, I am 80 years old, and I enjoy staying active by hiking and rowing. I have recently become interested in computers after learning more about how I can record my own weaving and knitting patterns using Excel. I think computers provide a wealth of new information and in the latter years of my life, I would like to learn a new skill to connect with my younger relatives such as my great-nieces.

My favorite colors are light pinks and purples, as you can see from the page. I enjoy weaving small blankets for my friend's many grandchildren, and I use light pastel colors.

In my younger days I was a park ranger in one of Canada's beautiful national parks, Banff. I am well-versed in map reading, rescue procedures, and how to correctly interact with wildlife. In my older years, I still try to participate in environmental activism.

I highly recommend you learn more about Canada's national parks, such as my favorite Banff and other wonderful parks such as Cape Breton Highlands
